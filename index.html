Brain Beats!

/

Ever had an idea that you know would be amazing, but could never perfectly replicate?

Well Brain Beats is here to solve that problem. Brain beats is a device that creates music based on brain waves in your brain to perfectly create the music you imagine in your mind.

Features!

    Tracks your favorite genres!
    Plays songs based on your mood!
    Collaborate with friends to make songs!
    And a lot more!

Questions frequently asked by shoppers!

How long does it take for Brain Beats to come up with a song for you?

Just a few minutes, but sometimes can take longer.

Do you feel anything when Brain Beats creates something?

You shouldn't but if you do please notify us immediately!

How much people does Brain Beats support in a collaboration?

Up to 10 people!
