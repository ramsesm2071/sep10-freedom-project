Current Inventions

/

Headphones! Lots of people use them!

Headphones are what everyone uses to listen to music, but they are easily replacable and often annoying to hold onto so people use things like airpods or earpods as they take up less space and do the same as headphones.

Brain Beats!

/

Ever had an idea that you know would be amazing, but could never perfectly replicate?

Well Brain Beats is here to solve that problem. Brain beats is a device that creates music based on brain waves in your brain to perfectly create the music you imagine in your mind rather than headphones that are annoying to carry and can only be used to listen to music.

Features!

    Tracks your favorite genres!
    Plays songs based on your mood!
    Collaborate with friends to make songs!
    And a lot more!
